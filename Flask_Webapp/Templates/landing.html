Social Media Deepfakes

Social media platforms have become fertile ground for the spread of deepfakes, which are synthetic media in which a person in an existing image or video is replaced with someone else's likeness. These deepfakes can be used for parody, satire, or malicious intent, blurring the line between reality and fabrication.
